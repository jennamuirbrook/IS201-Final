More About My Reading Journey

I have been an avid reader since childhood when I would stay up late at night, tucked between my bed covers, with a flashlight in my hand as I turned the pages of a new favorite book. Reading has shaped who I am and who I am becoming. I firmly believe that to learn to think, you must first learn to read.
